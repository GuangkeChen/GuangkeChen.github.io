About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor and Doctoral Supervisor at the Institute for Math & AI and the School of Computer Science, Wuhan University (opens new window). I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Stony Brook University (opens new window) (State University of New York at Stony Brook), working with the Empire Innovation Scholar Professor Ting Wang (opens new window), and was a Research Assistant Professor at Pengcheng Laboratory.

I am actively looking for self-motivated Ph.D , Master, and Undergarduate students to join my team. If you are interested, feel free to contact me!
